Reset

This project is an experimnent in inserting the internet interface into the critique of the racial politics of the global fertility market. As you click on the image-words, the data is stored on your device and visibilised in realtime through fluctuating valves that follow pre-assigned meanings (money or other standards of value). You can press ‘reset’ to set them back to 0.

This site uses local storage not to surveil you or play you but to play with you.

This is not quite a cookies ‘accept/reject’ button either, just sharing information about the tools used.
